# Social Posts

## What It Is

A place to draft and organize social media posts — this is a **composer and tracker**, not a publisher. IPRO doesn't connect to Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, or X directly; there's no login to any of those platforms and no automatic posting. You write the post once in IPRO, check it against each platform's character limit, then copy it and paste it into that platform's own app or website yourself.

## Write a Post

1. Select **Social Posts** in the Agent Portal menu.
2. Click **New Post**.
3. Optionally give it a topic (for your own organization, e.g. "Spring promo").
4. Write the post text. As you type, four counters show your length against each platform's real limit:
   - **X (Twitter)**: 280 characters
   - **Instagram**: 2,200 characters
   - **LinkedIn**: ~3,000 characters (practical limit)
   - **Facebook**: effectively unlimited
   A counter turns yellow near the limit and red once you're over it.
5. Click **Copy to Clipboard**, then paste the text into whichever platform you're posting to.
6. Click **Save Post** to keep it in your list.

## Draft with AI

Enter a topic, then click **Draft with AI** next to it to get a short starting post you can edit — professional tone, at most one emoji, no more than two hashtags, sized to fit comfortably under X's 280-character limit. It's a starting point, not a finished post: always read it over before saving or posting. This is a Platinum/Broker package feature; on Silver or Gold the button explains the upgrade needed instead of drafting.

## Track What's Been Posted

Every saved post starts as **Draft**. Once you've actually posted it somewhere, click **Mark Posted** on that post — this is self-reported (IPRO has no way to verify a real post happened), but it keeps your list organized between what's still pending and what's already gone out. Filter the list by Draft, Posted, or All.

## Edit or Delete

Click **Edit** on any post to change its topic or text. Click the trash icon to delete a post permanently.

Writing and tracking posts is included in every package; drafting with AI requires Platinum or Broker (see above).
